# Vocabulary & Ingredient Audit (draft v1)

Reviewable input for the pairing-expansion pipeline. **Nothing here is committed to the app
data yet.** Edit freely — this file becomes the source of truth for:
- the canonical ingredient vocabulary,
- the synonym map (used to normalize messy recipe strings during mining),
- the list of ingredients to add / connect.

Current state: **579 profiles, 15,615 pairings**, all tagged source `flavorbible`.

Legend for decisions: ✅ = proposed default, ❓ = needs your call.

---
